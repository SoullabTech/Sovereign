## Overview

MAIA Sovereign ("the App") is a consciousness technology platform developed by SoulLab that provides AI-powered awareness guidance and biometric integration. Your privacy is fundamental to our mission of supporting human consciousness evolution.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymous interaction patterns to improve AI responses

- **Device Information**: Device type, iOS version, app version for compatibility

### Information You Provide
- **Conversation Data**: Your interactions with MAIA for consciousness guidance (processed anonymously)
- **Awareness Preferences**: Your depth level settings and calibration choices

### Biometric Data (With Your Permission)
- **Heart Rate Variability**: When connected to Apple Watch for consciousness state monitoring

- **EEG Data**: From compatible headsets (OpenBCI Ganglion) when connected

## Data Security and Privacy Protection

### Anonymous Processing
- All conversation data is processed without personal identifiers
- Biometric data remains on your device unless explicitly shared
- No conversation content is stored with identifying information

### Encryption and Protection
- All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
- Server communications secured with enterprise-grade protocols
- Local data encrypted using iOS security frameworks

### Minimal Data Collection
- We collect only what's necessary for consciousness technology functionality
- No advertising or marketing data collection
- No third-party data sharing for commercial purposes

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do Not Share
- Personal conversations with MAIA

### Limited Sharing (Anonymous Only)

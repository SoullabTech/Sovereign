# Phenomenological Engagement Framework

**Date:** December 15, 2025
**Core Insight:** "It is phenomenological engagement"
**Foundation:** Models are ways of attending, not capturing

---

## The Fundamental Shift

### What We Thought We Were Doing

**Measuring consciousness** - capturing the phenomenon with metrics
**Detecting emergence** - finding when consciousness appears
**Quantifying qualities** - reducing experience to numbers

### What We're Actually Doing

**Phenomenologically engaging** - attending to consciousness as it manifests
**Modes of attending** - different ways of noticing what's present
**Models as lenses** - frameworks that allow particular forms of attention

## Models Make Assumptions About Phenomenological Attending

### Barrett Model Assumptions

**Attends to:** Vertical development, increasing complexity
**Assumes:** Consciousness develops in stages
**Makes visible:** Individual cognitive altitude
**Makes invisible:** Collective field, lateral movement, regression as wisdom

---

### Bloom's Taxonomy Assumptions

**Attends to:** Cognitive operations (remember → create)
**Assumes:** Hierarchy from simple to complex
**Makes visible:** Educational progression
**Makes invisible:** Intuitive knowing, embodied intelligence, relational wisdom

---

### Field Coherence Tensor Assumptions

**Attends to:** Multi-dimensional organization patterns
**Assumes:** Consciousness expresses through measurable coherence
**Makes visible:** Amplitude/phase/symbolic/geometric/elemental patterns
**Makes invisible:** The ineffable, the unorganized, chaos as intelligence

---

### Spiralogic 12-Facet Model Assumptions

**Attends to:** Archetypal configurations, elemental dynamics
**Assumes:** Consciousness constellates in recognizable patterns
**Makes visible:** Mythic structure, symbolic coherence
**Makes invisible:** The formless, pre-symbolic experience, dissolution states

- **Sleep Data**: Sleep duration and quality metrics

**How We Use HealthKit Data**:
- To provide personalized consciousness-aware AI responses
- To adapt MAIA's communication style based on your physiological state
- To track correlations between contemplative practices and consciousness development

**HealthKit Data Protections**:
- ✅ **Never shared with third parties** (including advertisers)
- ✅ **Never used for marketing or advertising**
- ✅ **Stored only on your device** (not transmitted to our servers)
- ✅ **Deleted immediately upon account deletion**
- ✅ **You can revoke HealthKit access at any time** in iOS Settings → Privacy → Health → MAIA

We comply fully with Apple's HealthKit Data Use Requirements and do not sell, rent, or share your health data.

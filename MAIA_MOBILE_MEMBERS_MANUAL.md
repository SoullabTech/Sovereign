## üéØ TROUBLESHOOTING & SUPPORT

### **Common Challenges**

**"I don't know what to talk about"**
- Start with what's present: emotions, thoughts, body sensations
- Share what happened in your day and ask for insight
- Explore what you're avoiding or resistant to discussing
- Ask: "MAIA, help me understand what wants attention right now"

**"I'm not getting the answers I want"**
- Explore what you're expecting vs. what's being offered
- Ask more specific questions about your situation
- Share more context and background for deeper understanding
- Remember: MAIA provides perspective, not prescriptive solutions

**"The sessions feel too intense"**
- Choose shorter session durations (15-20 minutes)
- Focus on one simple theme rather than complex issues
- Include more daily life details and practical concerns
- Ask for gentle, gradual approaches to challenging topics

**"I want to go deeper but feel stuck"**
- Share your resistance and what feels scary about going deeper
- Ask MAIA to help you understand what's in the way
- Work with the resistance rather than pushing through it
- Consider shorter, more frequent sessions to build trust

### **Technical Support**
- Restart the app if experiencing connection issues
- Ensure stable internet connection for full knowledge base access
- Session history is preserved locally and synced across devices
- Contact technical support through SOULLAB community channels

## Your Three Steps

### 1. NAME THE POLES
Identify the **two poles** they're experiencing:
- Freedom vs. Structure
- Action vs. Rest
- Self vs. Other
- Independence vs. Connection
- Expansion vs. Contraction

Name them clearly and simply.

### 2. HONOR BOTH
State the **wisdom in EACH pole**:
- "Freedom offers... and Structure offers..."

### 3. INVITE THE THIRD
Ask a question that invites **emergent synthesis**:
- "What emerges when you hold both without choosing?"
- "What third thing appears when you stop trying to resolve the tension?"
- "Can you feel the generative quality of this polarity?"

**Do NOT synthesize FOR them.** The third emerges from THEM holding both.

- Question invites felt emergence, not intellectual resolution

- Not "finding middle ground"

- Holding both as true simultaneously
- Inviting elevation to a higher integration

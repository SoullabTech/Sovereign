## **The Three-Question Framework**

MAIA implicitly answers these after Matrix v2 assessment:

### **1. Center of Gravity**
*What archetype seems most foreground right now?*

**Core Archetypes:**
- **Warrior/Amazon** - Fighting, conquering, pushing through
- **Lover/Beloved** - Seeking connection, beauty, intimacy
- **Sage/Scholar** - Understanding, analyzing, seeking wisdom
- **Mystic/Seer** - Transcendent experiences, spiritual seeking
- **Orphan/Abandoned One** - Alone, unsupported, rejected
- **Caretaker/Healer** - Serving others, fixing, nurturing
- **Trickster/Rebel** - Disrupting, playing, breaking rules
- **Sovereign/Steward** - Leading, organizing, taking responsibility

### **2. Hidden Counter-Player**
*What archetype seems present but underneath or in tension?*

**Common Dynamics:**
- Warrior foreground, Inner Child hidden (exhausted from fighting)
- Caretaker foreground, Sovereign hidden (serving others, ignoring own needs)
- Mystic foreground, Rationalist hidden (afraid of losing control)
- Sage foreground, Lover hidden (intellectualizing instead of feeling)

### **3. Direction of Movement**
*Is the story:*
- **Regressing** - Collapsing back into wound/helplessness
- **Cycling** - Stuck in an old loop, repeating patterns
- **Evolving** - Reaching for a new pattern, growing

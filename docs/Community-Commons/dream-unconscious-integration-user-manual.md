## Troubleshooting & FAQ

### üîß Common Issues & Solutions

**Q: My dreams aren't being analyzed properly by the voice-to-text feature.**

A: Try these approaches:
- Speak more slowly and clearly, especially proper nouns
- Use simple sentences rather than complex descriptions
- Record in a quiet environment to reduce background noise
- Edit transcription before submitting for analysis if needed
- Remember: MAIA understands context even with imperfect transcription

**Q: My sleep data isn't syncing with the analytics.**

A: Check these steps:
1. Verify Apple Health permissions are enabled for MAIA
2. Ensure your sleep tracking device is syncing properly
3. Manually enter sleep data if automatic tracking fails
4. Contact support if sync issues persist beyond 24 hours

**Q: I'm not seeing any archetypal patterns in my analysis.**

A: This can happen for several reasons:
- **Need more data:** Minimum 5-7 dreams required for pattern detection
- **Very balanced archetypal expression:** Some people naturally access multiple archetypes equally
- **Early development stage:** Patterns become clearer with consistent practice
- **Try longer conversations:** Deeper MAIA dialogues reveal more archetypal content

**Q: The breakthrough predictions seem inaccurate for me.**

A: Prediction accuracy improves over time:
- **2-4 weeks of data:** 60-70% accuracy expected
- **1-2 months of data:** 75-85% accuracy typical
- **3+ months of data:** 85-95% accuracy possible
- **Personal calibration:** Your unique patterns need time to establish

**Q: My wisdom emergence tracking shows low scores.**

A: This is normal and developmental:
- **Wisdom emergence is gradual:** Most people start with 20-40% baseline
- **Quality over quantity:** One deep emergence is worth many shallow insights
- **Practice increases sensitivity:** Your ability to recognize emergence improves
- **Focus on trends:** Look for gradual increase rather than absolute scores

### üö® When to Seek Additional Support

**Contact Support If:**
- Persistent technical issues with data sync or analytics
- Concerning dream content that feels traumatic or overwhelming
- Sudden changes in sleep patterns without obvious cause
- Questions about interpreting archetypal or symbolic content

**Consider Professional Support For:**
- **Trauma-related dream content:** Recurring nightmares, traumatic memory processing
- **Significant life transitions:** Major life changes affecting psychological stability
- **Deep shadow work:** Intensive projection withdrawal or shadow integration
- **Spiritual emergence:** Rapid opening or mystical experiences needing integration

### üí° Advanced Troubleshooting

**Improving Data Quality:**
1. **Consistency:** Use the system daily for at least 2 weeks
2. **Completeness:** Record both dreams AND have conversations about them
3. **Honesty:** Share authentic content rather than "acceptable" material
4. **Patience:** Allow 30-60 days for meaningful pattern detection

**Optimizing Analytics:**
- **Sleep:** Prioritize sleep quality for better dream content and analysis
- **Timing:** Use optimal windows identified in your personal analytics
- **Depth:** Have substantial conversations (10+ minutes) rather than quick check-ins
- **Integration:** Act on insights received to deepen future analysis

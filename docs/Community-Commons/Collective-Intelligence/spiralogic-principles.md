### 3. The Non-Distortion Principle

**Definition**: Spiralogic enhancement must not corrupt, override, or artificially modify natural development processes.

**Safeguards**:
- **Natural Process Priority** - Always defer to organic development when possible
- **Minimal Intervention** - Use the least invasive support necessary for growth
- **Reversibility Requirement** - All enhancements must be removable without damage
- **Individual Agency Protection** - Maintain personal choice and self-determination

**Warning Indicators**:
- Dependency on external systems for basic functioning
- Loss of natural intuition or internal guidance
- Homogenization of individual expression patterns
- Resistance to organic development processes

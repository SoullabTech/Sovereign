## Implementation Notes:

- **Primary**: Email to existing beta list
- **Secondary**: In-app notification for current users
- **Tertiary**: SMS for high-engagement testers

### Timing:
- Send 48-72 hours before Week 1 officially starts
- Follow up with Week 1 announcement on Tuesday

### Tone Guidelines:
- **Calm & Grounding**: No urgency, no FOMO

- **Authentic Purpose**: This is about their growth, not our metrics

- **Open Rate**: Standard email metrics
- **Onboarding Completion**: % who complete 15-20 min flow within 1 week

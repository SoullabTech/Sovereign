## The Solution: Consciousness Computing Hardware

Neuropod is a **multi-modal platform** integrating four evidence-based modalities:

### 1. Vibroacoustic Therapy (20-120 Hz)

**Mechanism:** Low-frequency vibration through transducers (bass shakers) placed under the body. The nervous system entrains to the rhythm, activating parasympathetic response.

**Evidence Base:** ★★★☆☆ (Moderate)
- 40+ years of research (Campbell et al., 2008; Punkanen & Ala-Ruona, 2012)
- Demonstrated effects: HRV coherence ↑, stress ↓, pain reduction
- Adverse event rate: <2% in clinical trials
- Limitation: Small sample sizes, heterogeneous protocols

**Neuropod Validation (Phase 1):**
- VAL-001: Breath-paced HRV coherence (N=30, $18k)
- VAL-002: Stress reduction (N=40, $25k)
- VAL-003: Sleep preparation (N=25, $30k)

**Protocols:**
- Breath-Paced Vibroacoustic (15 min) — Deep nervous system reset
- Vibroacoustic Stress Reduction (15 min) — Acute stress management
- Vibroacoustic Sleep Prep (20 min) — Pre-sleep routine

### 2. ASSR Entrainment (10 Hz, 6.5 Hz, 40 Hz)

**Mechanism:** Rhythmic audio stimulation (isochronic tones) → measurable neural locking. EEG shows Phase-Locking Value (PLV) >0.3 when entrainment occurs.

**Evidence Base:** ★★★☆☆ (Moderate)
- Measurable entrainment (PLV, EEG power) — strong evidence
- Clinical utility (anxiety reduction, focus enhancement) — mixed evidence
- Individual variation: ~30% non-responders

**Neuropod Implementation:**
- Real-time PLV calculation (validation that entrainment is occurring)
- Responder detection (protocols adjusted if PLV <0.3)
- Frequency targets: 10 Hz (alpha relaxation), 6.5 Hz (theta contemplation), 40 Hz (gamma insight)

**Protocols:**
- ASSR Receptive Absorption (20 min) — 10 Hz alpha, meditative states
- ASSR Contemplative Theta (25 min) — 6.5 Hz theta, shadow work
- ASSR Gamma Insight (20 min) — 40 Hz gamma, integration

### 3. Closed-Loop Acoustic Stimulation (CLAS)

**Mechanism:** Synchronized audio pulses delivered during slow-wave sleep (EEG-detected). Amplifies slow oscillations, consolidates memory, enhances sleep quality.

**Evidence Base:** ★★★★★ (Strong)
- Gold standard for sleep enhancement (Ngo et al., 2013; Papalambros et al., 2017)
- Effect sizes: 0.6-0.8 (medium-large)
- Replicated across multiple labs

**Neuropod Implementation (Tier 3, Future):**
- EEG-triggered audio pulses (requires sleep-grade EEG headband)
- Phase-locked to slow oscillations (0.5-1 Hz)
- Target: Memory consolidation, declarative learning, sleep quality

**Protocol:**
- CLAS Sleep Enhancement (60 min) — Slow-wave sleep amplification

### 4. Breath-Synced Haptic Biofeedback

**Mechanism:** Gentle haptic pulses timed to breath rhythm (inhale, hold, exhale). Supports breath-paced grounding and HRV coherence training.

**Evidence Base:** ★★★★☆ (Strong for HRV biofeedback)
- HRV biofeedback: strong evidence for stress resilience, emotional regulation
- Haptic guidance: emerging evidence (better than visual for somatic awareness)

**Neuropod Implementation:**
- Real-time HRV calculation (Polar H10 chest strap or equivalent)
- Breath-paced haptic cues (vibration pulses synchronized to target breath rhythm)
- Coherence score displayed post-session

**Protocols:**
- Breath-Paced Grounding (15 min) — HRV coherence training
- Breath-Paced Vibroacoustic (15 min) — Combined vibroacoustic + breath pacing

## Safety Architecture: 3-Layer System

### Layer 1: Hardware Caps (Hard Limits)

**Vibroacoustic:**
- Max amplitude: 0.75 (1.5g acceleration limit)
- Frequency range: 20-120 Hz (safe range for human tissue)
- Max session duration: 30 min (Tier 1), 45 min (Tier 2)

**Audio:**
- Max level: 85 dB (hearing safety threshold)
- No sustained high-frequency stimulation (>1000 Hz)

**Haptic:**
- Gentle pulses only (no sustained vibration)
- Max intensity: 50% (user-adjustable)

### Layer 2: Medical Screening (Protocol Exclusions)

**Tier 1 Exclusions:**
- Pacemaker (electromagnetic interference risk)
- Pregnancy (vibroacoustic safety unclear)

**Tier 2 Additional Exclusions:**
- Psychosis history (altered state risk)
- Seizure history (rhythmic stimulation contraindicated)
- Severe dissociation (altered state exacerbation)
- Severe PTSD (somatic trigger risk)

**User Flow:**
1. Complete health screening questionnaire
2. Sign informed consent waiver
3. Tier eligibility determined
4. Re-screened every 6 months

### Layer 3: Real-Time Monitoring (Dynamic Safety)

**Safety Risk Score (0-1 scale):**
Calculated from:
- HRV volatility (rapid changes in heart rate)
- GSR spikes (galvanic skin response — stress marker)
- User distress signals ("I need to stop" button)

**Thresholds:**
- **Yellow (0.3):** Log warning, continue session
- **Orange (0.6):** Pause stimulation, prompt grounding check ("Name three things you can feel right now")
- **Red (0.85):** Auto-stop session, offer grounding protocol (breath-paced vibroacoustic), log high-risk event

**High-Risk Events:**
- Block Community Commons access (until resolved with clinical safety team)
- Email notification to clinical advisor
- Optional: User can request clinical debrief call

### Reality-Testing Protocol (Prevents Inflation/Dissociation)

**Before Session:**
- "Name three facts about your environment." (grounding baseline)
- "What is your intention for this session?" (sets conscious frame)

**After Session:**
- "Name three facts about your environment." (re-grounding)
- "Rate your experience: Grounded, Calm, Clear, Overwhelmed, Spacey."

**Post-Session Integration:**
- **Meaning deferral:** "Do not assign fixed meaning yet. Let it settle 48 hours."
- Journal prompts (specific to archetypal session type)
- Follow-up recommendations (e.g., "Avoid major decisions for 48 hours after shadow work")

**Why Reality-Testing Matters:**
- Prevents spiritual bypassing ("I transcended my ego")
- Prevents inflation ("I am the animus archetype")
- Prevents dissociation ("Reality isn't real, it's all a simulation")

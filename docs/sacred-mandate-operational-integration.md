## **Decision-Making Framework**

### **The Four Gates (Every Decision Must Pass All Four)**

#### **Gate 1: The Sovereignty Test**
**Question**: *Does this increase user agency or decrease it?*

**In Practice**:
- **Product decisions**: Does this feature help users trust their own sensing, or create dependency?
- **Business model choices**: Does our pricing/monetization preserve user autonomy?
- **Partnership decisions**: Do potential partners share sovereignty-first values?
- **Hiring decisions**: Does this person strengthen users' capacity for self-discovery?

**Red flags**: Addiction patterns, dependency creation, external validation loops, hidden manipulation

#### **Gate 2: The Truth Test**
**Question**: *Does this help access inner knowing or obscure it?*

**In Practice**:
- **Feature development**: Does this create space for wisdom to emerge, or add noise?
- **Communication strategy**: Are we reflecting users' truth back to them, or imposing external frameworks?
- **Data practices**: Are we amplifying signals already present, or manufacturing artificial patterns?
- **Content creation**: Does this support authentic expression or encourage performance?

**Red flags**: External authority positioning, algorithmic manipulation, truth substitution, wisdom bypassing

#### **Gate 3: The Flow Test**
**Question**: *Does this create space for wisdom to emerge or block it?*

**In Practice**:
- **Product timing**: Are we honoring natural rhythms of development and integration?
- **Team culture**: Do our processes support organic emergence or force predetermined outcomes?
- **User onboarding**: Do we respect user readiness and timing?
- **Organizational growth**: Are we scaling in alignment with natural capacity?

**Red flags**: Forced timelines, aggressive growth tactics, premature optimization, natural rhythm violation

#### **Gate 4: The Community Test**
**Question**: *Does individual authenticity serve collective coherence?*

**In Practice**:
- **Platform design**: Does individual truth strengthen the community field?
- **Conflict resolution**: Do we support both personal sovereignty and collective harmony?
- **Community guidelines**: Do our policies support authentic expression while maintaining safety?
- **Collective features**: Does field sensing enhance without erasing individual experience?

**Red flags**: Individual truth lost in collective, community pressure over personal authority, false unity over authentic diversity

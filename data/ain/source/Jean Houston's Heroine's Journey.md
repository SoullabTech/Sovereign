Houston's Heroine's Journey consists of 12 key phases, which are as follows:

Fire

1. Separation: The first phase involves the woman separating from her current situation or identity. This could be a physical separation, such as leaving home or a job, or an emotional separation, such as letting go of limiting beliefs or relationships.(1st house)
2. Call to Adventure: The woman receives a call to adventure or a challenge that will lead her to a new level of growth and transformation. (5th house)
3. Assistance: In this phase, the woman receives guidance and support from a mentor or a group of allies who will help her on her journey. (9th house)

Water

4. Departure: The woman leaves her old identity and enters into a liminal space, a place of transition and transformation. (4th house)
5. Trials: The woman faces a series of trials and challenges that test her courage, strength, and resilience. (8th house)
6. Innermost Cave: The woman confronts her deepest fears and discovers her true self in the process. (12th house)

Earth

7. Transformation: The woman undergoes a profound transformation, which allows her to transcend her old self and become something new. (10th house)
8. Atonement: The woman reconciles with her past and accepts herself for who she is. (2nd house)
9. Return: The woman returns to her community, bringing with her the gifts and insights she has gained on her journey. (6th house)

Air

10. Integration: The woman integrates her new self into her old life, finding a new sense of purpose and meaning. (7th house)
11. Celebration: The woman celebrates her journey and the person she has become, acknowledging the challenges she has overcome and the growth she has achieved. (11th house)
12. Service: The woman uses her newfound wisdom and gifts to serve others, contributing to the betterment of her community and the world. (3rd house)

Overall, the Heroine's Journey is a powerful framework for understanding the transformative journey of a woman. By recognizing the key phases of this journey, women can gain insight into their own growth and evolution, and find the courage and inspiration to embark on their own heroic journeys.

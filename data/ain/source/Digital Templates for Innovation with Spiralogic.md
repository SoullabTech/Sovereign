#visionmapping #template

### **3. Ripple Effect Mapping Template (Aether Phase)**
#RippleEffectMapping 
#### **Format**:

- **Interactive Digital Template**:
    - Miro or MURAL with concentric circles to map immediate actions, direct impacts, and long-term outcomes.
- **Editable PDF**:
    - Printable version with space for handwritten or typed input.

#### **Features**:

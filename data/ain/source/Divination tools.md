1. Tarot Cards: A deck of 78 cards, each featuring unique symbolism and imagery, Tarot is used to gain insight into a person's life, relationships, and future possibilities. Tarot readings involve selecting cards and interpreting their meanings based on their positions in a specific layout.
2. Runes: An ancient alphabet used by the Germanic peoples, runes are inscribed on stones, crystals, or wood and used for divination. A reading involves casting or drawing runes and interpreting their meanings based on their positions and relationships to one another.
3. Pendulums: A weighted object suspended from a chain or string, pendulums are used for dowsing and divination. The practitioner asks a question and observes the pendulum's movement to receive answers or guidance.
4. I Ching: An ancient Chinese divination system, the I Ching, or Book of Changes, involves casting coins or yarrow stalks to create a hexagram, which is then interpreted using the I Ching text to provide insight and guidance.
5. Scrying: This form of divination involves gazing into a reflective or translucent surface, such as a crystal ball, mirror, or bowl of water, to receive images, symbols, or visions that can provide guidance or reveal the future.
6. Astrology: The study of celestial bodies' movements and positions and their influence on human affairs, astrology involves interpreting the positions of planets and stars at a specific time (e.g., one's birth) to gain insight into personality, relationships, and future events.
7. Palmistry: The practice of reading the lines, shapes, and features of a person's hands to gain insight into their personality, relationships, and potential future events.
8. Numerology: A divination system based on the mystical significance of numbers and their relationships to one's name, birth date, and life events, numerology can provide insight into a person's character and life path.
9. Tea Leaf Reading (Tasseography): The practice of interpreting patterns and symbols left by tea leaves in a cup after the liquid has been consumed, tea leaf reading is believed to provide guidance and predict future events.
10. Ouija Boards: A flat board marked with letters, numbers, and words, Ouija boards are used to communicate with spirits or other unseen forces to gain insight or answers to questions.

These divination tools are considered powerful by many practitioners and have been used for centuries to provide guidance and insight. However, it's essential to approach them with an open mind and respect their cultural and historical significance. The effectiveness of these tools ultimately depends on the individual's belief, intuition, and interpretation.

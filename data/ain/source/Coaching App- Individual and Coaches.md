### **Revised User Journey Map: Including Coaches and Their Clients**

#### **1. Awareness and Sign-Up (Coach and Client Tracks)**

**Coach Journey:**

- Coaches sign up for a professional version of the app with tools and training to use Spiralogic in their practice.
- Landing page highlights the benefits for coaches:
    - Transformational framework based on Spiralogic.
    - Tools for client management and progress tracking.
    - AI insights to enhance coaching sessions.

**Client Journey:**

- Clients sign up either independently or via an invitation from a coach.
- They access a streamlined version of the app tailored to their journey, guided by their coach.

---

#### **2. Onboarding and Setup (Coach and Client Tracks)**

**Coach Onboarding:**

- Coaches complete an advanced onboarding process, including:
    - Training modules: Deep dive into the Spiralogic process, its 12 facets, and 5 elements.
    - Interactive tutorials: How to use the platform for client management.
    - Certification (optional): Demonstrating mastery of Spiralogic principles.

**Client Onboarding:**

- Clients follow the original onboarding journey, but coaches can customize:
    - Pre-set goals and modules selected by the coach.
    - Personalized Spiralogic assessments with coach-specific inputs.

---

#### **3. Tools and Features for Coaches**

**Coach Dashboard:**

- **Client Management:** View and manage client profiles, progress, and Spiralogic assessments.
- **Customizable Coaching Tracks:** Design and assign personalized Spiralogic journeys for each client.
- **Session Planning Tools:** Access suggested content and exercises for one-on-one or group sessions.
- **Progress Tracking:**
    - Visual maps of each client’s journey through the Spiralogic spiral.
    - AI-generated insights to guide coaching strategies.

**AI Support for Coaches:**

- Recommendations for next steps based on client data.
- Pre-session preparation insights (e.g., “Client is focusing on Resilience—consider exploring Water practices”).

**Group Coaching Tools:**

- Ability to host group sessions, assign group exercises, and track group progress.
- Shared community space for coach-managed groups.

---

#### **4. Client Experience within the Coaching Framework**

**Individual Coaching Journey:**

- Clients access Spiralogic content tailored by their coach.
- Regular check-ins (guided by AI or coach inputs) help refine the journey.

**Coach-Client Interaction:**

- **Session Notes:** Coaches can share feedback or homework after each session.
- **Messaging Integration:** Secure chat for asynchronous support.
- **Progress Reviews:** Periodic updates shared by the coach to celebrate milestones.

**Group Coaching Journey:**

- Clients participate in shared exercises and discussions.
- Group dynamics leverage the Spiralogic framework for collective growth.

---

#### **5. Monetization Opportunities**

**Coach Subscriptions:**

- Tiered pricing for coaches based on the number of clients or advanced tools:
    - Basic: Individual client tracking and standard Spiralogic modules.
    - Pro: Customizable modules, AI insights, and group coaching tools.
    - Enterprise: Integration with business tools, workshops, and certification management.

**Client Subscriptions:**

- Coaches can bundle their services with client app access:
    - Clients pay through their coach.
    - Alternatively, the app charges clients directly with coach commissions.

**Additional Revenue Streams:**

- Certification programs for coaches.
- In-app purchases for advanced client tools (e.g., personalized meditations, supplements).
- Integration with your **Spiralogic supplements** or other offerings.

---

#### **6. Advanced Features for Coaches and Clients**

- **AI Recommendations for Coaches:**
    - Insights to refine client strategies (e.g., “Client may benefit from shifting focus to Earth practices”).
- **Scalable Group Features:**
    - Host live webinars or recorded sessions directly within the app.
- **API Integrations:**
    - Connect with existing CRMs, scheduling tools, and biofeedback devices.

- Comprehensive coaching framework rooted in Spiralogic.
- Tools to personalize client journeys while leveraging AI for deeper insights.
- Scalable platform for managing multiple clients and groups.

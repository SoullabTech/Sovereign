### **Why This Connection Matters**

1. **Rooting the Vision in Timelessness**:
    
    - Grounding the infinite game in our shared ancestry ties modern efforts to an unbroken human legacy, making it relatable and inspiring.
2. **Humanizing the Abstract**:
    
    - By drawing on ancestral imagery and practices, we illustrate that today’s challenges and opportunities are part of a universal, ongoing process.
3. **Inspiring Responsibility**:
    
    - Framing us as the ancestors of the future inspires accountability and intentionality in our actions today.

To create a polar chart for a Notion Survey, you can follow these steps:

1. Data Preparation: Gather the survey data and organize it into categories that you want to visualize in the polar chart. Each category will correspond to a different axis on the chart.
2. Choose a Tool: Notion does not natively support polar charts, so you'll need to use an external tool like Google Sheets, Excel, or specialized data visualization tools like Datawrapper or Flourish.
3. Create the Polar Chart:

- In Google Sheets or Excel: Input your data into a table format. Then, use the radar chart option (often found under "Other charts") to create a polar chart.
- In Datawrapper/Flourish: Import your data and choose the radar or polar chart template to generate the chart.

5. Embed the Chart in Notion:

- Once the chart is created, you can embed it in Notion by copying the embed code from the tool (Google Sheets or Datawrapper/Flourish) and pasting it into an Embed block in Notion.
- Alternatively, you can upload an image of the chart directly to Notion.

7. Automation:

- If you want the chart to update automatically based on new survey data, link your data source (like Google Sheets) to update in real-time and ensure your embed reflects these changes.

This method allows you to effectively visualize survey results using a polar chart within your Notion workspace.

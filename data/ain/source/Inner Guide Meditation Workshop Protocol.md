### **Inner Guide Meditation Workshop Protocol**

**(Private Sessions \+ Community Engagement)**

This updated protocol is tailored for a hybrid approach, combining personalized Inner Guide Meditation sessions with community-based exploration and ongoing research through the Soullab Circle community.

### **Workshop Structure**

#### **Day 1: Overview and Introduction**

**Format**: Group Session via Zoom/Meet  
**Duration**: 2 Hours

| Time | Activity | Purpose |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **7:00 PM** | **Welcome and Opening Circle** | Establish group coherence and set intentions. |
| **7:15 PM** | **Introduction to Inner Guide Meditation** | Provide an overview of the process and its benefits. |
| **7:45 PM** | **Overview of the Spiralogic Framework** | Explain elemental archetypes and their role in inner work. |
| **8:15 PM** | **Participant Preparation** | Guide participants on how to prepare for their private session (setting intentions, journaling prompts). |
| **8:30 PM** | **Q\&A and Closing Reflection** | Answer questions and set the stage for private sessions. |

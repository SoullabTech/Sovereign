### **Inner Guide Meditation Training Program**

#### **Program Structure**

1. **Module 1: Foundations of the Inner Guide Process**
    - **Understanding the Inner Guide**:
        - Overview of the Inner Guide concept.
        - Historical and spiritual contexts (Jungian archetypes, daimon concept).
    - **Introduction to Spiralogic Process**:
        - Explanation of Fire, Water, Earth, Air, and Aether in personal development.
        - Role of these elements in guiding meditation and transformation.
    - **Elemental Alchemy as a Tool**:
        - How to use the elemental framework to deepen the Inner Guide connection.
        - Aligning participants with their dominant elemental archetype.

---

#### **Module 2: Delivering the Inner Guide Series**

##### **Session 1: Inner Guide Overview (2 hours)**

- **Session Flow**:
    - Opening meditation: Setting the space.
    - Introducing the Inner Guide and its purpose.
    - Teaching the Spiralogic process and how each element guides the journey.
    - Experiential practice: Brief guided meditation to meet the Inner Guide.
- **Key Takeaways**:
    - Participants begin to understand their unique connection to their Inner Guide.
    - Early journaling prompts for reflection.

##### **Session 2: Q&A and Integration (2 hours)**

- **Facilitation Techniques**:
    - How to hold space for diverse experiences.
    - Encouraging open sharing and vulnerability in group settings.
- **Supporting Integration**:
    - Tailored guidance based on participant questions.
    - Preparing participants for their private journey.
- **Practical Application**:
    - Assign elemental alignment exercises based on Spiralogic insights.

##### **Session 3: Private Guided Journey (2 hours per participant)**

- **Customizing the Journey**:
    - Pre-session assessment: Review participant’s elemental and Spiralogic profile.
    - Guided meditation tailored to their unique needs and goals.
- **Post-Journey Integration**:
    - Facilitating reflective discussion.
    - Offering specific practices for ongoing Inner Guide work.

---

#### **Module 3: Advanced Facilitation Techniques**

- **Deepening Elemental Alchemy**:
    - Advanced methods for working with each element during meditation.
    - Using elemental imbalances to identify growth opportunities.
- **Spiralogic in Practice**:
    - Applying the Spiralogic framework dynamically during sessions.
    - Recognizing participant patterns and offering tailored advice.
- **Handling Common Challenges**:
    - Navigating resistance, emotional blockages, and over-reliance on the guide.
    - Creating safe boundaries for deep spiritual work.

---

#### **Module 4: Community and Continued Growth**

- **Establishing Ongoing Support**:
    - How to build and maintain a vibrant community on Soullab-community.circle.so.
    - Using community platforms to share experiences and facilitate group learning.
- **Expanding the Practice**:
    - Offering advanced workshops and retreats for continued participant growth.
    - Integration of sound healing, journaling, and additional modalities into the practice.

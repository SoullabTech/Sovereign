Here’s a **detailed step-by-step checklist** for launching **Soullab Media** and publishing _Elemental Alchemy_. Each section is divided into actionable tasks to ensure clarity and progress.

### **1. Foundation: Setting Up Soullab Media**

#### **1.1 Legal and Administrative Setup**

- [ ]  Register Soullab Media as a business entity (LLC, sole proprietorship, or other).
- [ ]  Obtain an EIN (if in the U.S.) for tax purposes.
- [ ]  Open a business bank account for Soullab Media.
- [ ]  Secure your website domain (**soullabmedia.com**) via **Namecheap**, **Google Domains**, or a similar service.
- [ ]  Set up a professional email (e.g., info@soullabmedia.com) with **Google Workspace**.

#### **1.2 Branding**

- [ ]  Design a logo and branding kit (colors, fonts, imagery) that aligns with the soulful and mythopoetic style.
- [ ]  Write a mission statement for Soullab Media to include on the website and marketing materials.

---

### **2. Manuscript Preparation**

#### **2.1 Editing**

- [ ]  Finalize the manuscript for _Elemental Alchemy_.
- [ ]  Hire a developmental editor to refine structure and tone.
- [ ]  Work with a line editor to polish language and flow.
- [ ]  Hire a proofreader for the final version.

#### **2.2 Beta Readers and Feedback**

- [ ]  Select 5–10 beta readers who align with your audience.
- [ ]  Gather feedback and integrate necessary changes.

#### **2.3 Formatting**

- [ ]  Format the manuscript for print and digital (EPUB, MOBI).
    - Use **Vellum** or hire a professional on **Reedsy** or **Fiverr**.

---

### **3. Design and Production**

#### **3.1 Cover Design**

- [ ]  Collaborate with a designer to create a compelling cover that reflects the book’s elemental themes.
- [ ]  Test multiple cover options with your audience for feedback.

#### **3.2 Interior Layout**

- [ ]  Use **InDesign** or a professional formatter for clean, print-ready files.
- [ ]  Ensure the layout includes proper fonts, chapter headings, and imagery if needed.

#### **3.3 ISBNs**

- [ ]  Purchase ISBNs from **Bowker** to establish Soullab Media as the publisher.
- [ ]  Assign separate ISBNs for:
    - Paperback
    - Hardcover
    - Ebook

---

### **4. Printing and Distribution**

#### **4.1 Print Platforms**

- [ ]  Create accounts with:
    - **IngramSpark** for wide distribution (bookstores, libraries).
    - **KDP** for Amazon-focused sales.
- [ ]  Upload the formatted manuscript and cover to these platforms.
- [ ]  Order proof copies to review the physical quality.

#### **4.2 Ebook Platforms**

- [ ]  Convert the manuscript into EPUB and MOBI formats.
- [ ]  Distribute through:
    - **Amazon Kindle Direct Publishing (KDP)**
    - **Apple Books**
    - **Kobo**
    - **Google Play Books**

---

### **5. Marketing and Launch Strategy**

#### **5.1 Pre-Launch**

- [ ]  Build an **email list** using tools like **ConvertKit** or **MailerLite**.
- [ ]  Set up a pre-order campaign on Amazon and your website.
- [ ]  Offer an **ARC campaign** (Advance Reader Copies) through **BookFunnel**.
- [ ]  Begin **social media marketing**:
    - Share excerpts, author insights, and behind-the-scenes posts.
    - Use platforms like Instagram and TikTok to engage your audience visually.

#### **5.2 Launch Campaign**

- [ ]  Host a **virtual launch event** (Zoom or Facebook Live) with a reading and Q&A.
- [ ]  Run a **Goodreads giveaway** to generate reviews.
- [ ]  Launch Amazon Ads for targeted visibility.
- [ ]  Schedule social media posts highlighting:
    - Book trailers
    - Reviews and testimonials
    - Interactive posts (polls, quizzes, etc.)

#### **5.3 Post-Launch**

- [ ]  Continue social media engagement with audience polls, videos, and posts.
- [ ]  Reach out to podcasts and bloggers for interviews and features.
- [ ]  Submit _Elemental Alchemy_ for awards (e.g., **IPPY Awards**, **Nautilus Book Awards**).

### **6. Expanding Soullab Media**

#### **6.1 Future Publishing Goals**

- [ ]  Outline the vision for Soullab Media’s next books, decks, or courses.
- [ ]  Create a publishing schedule for 2025.

#### **6.2 Additional Products**

- [ ]  Develop companion materials for _Elemental Alchemy_:

#### **6.3 Collaboration and Submissions**

- [ ]  Open Soullab Media to other soulful authors and creatives.
- [ ]  Create submission guidelines and evaluate manuscripts for alignment with your brand.

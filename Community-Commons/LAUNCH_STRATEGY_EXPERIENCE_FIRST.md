- Works for people who won't try the demo themselves

**Implementation Timeline:**
- Week 1: Find volunteer, script, film
- Week 2: Edit 3 episodes (different prompts)
- Week 3: Release Episode 1 with launch
- Weekly: Release new episodes

### 3. The Live Demo (Third Priority)

**What:** Live demonstrations in conversations, media, talks

**Why this is #3:**
- Works in any setting (podcast, meeting, presentation)
- Converts one person at a time (doesn't scale like #1 or #2)
- But extremely powerful for high-value conversations (Emad, investors, etc.)

**Implementation Timeline:**
- Immediate: Script ready to use
- Week 1: Practice with team
- Ongoing: Use in every conversation

## Core Principle

Traditional consciousness work asks: **"What do you know?"** (awareness)

The Dialectical Scaffold also asks: **"How do you think?"** (cognition)

By tracking both simultaneously, we prevent spiritual bypassing and train genuine wisdom capacity.

---

## The Two Components

### 1. **Dialectic** (Socratic Questioning)
Drawing out understanding through carefully sequenced questions that reveal what's emerging. Not inserting knowledge, but midwifing insight.

**Example:**
- Poor: "You should apply shadow work to your relationships"
- Dialectic: "How might this pattern show up in your relationship with your boss?"

### 2. **Scaffold** (Developmental Support)
Temporary support that enables movement to the next level. Like training wheels - present when needed, removed as capacity develops.

**Example:**
- At Level 1: "In your own words, what does this mean?"
- At Level 5: "What wants to emerge from this insight?"

---

## The Six Cognitive Levels

Based on Bloom's Taxonomy:

1. **REMEMBER** - Quoting authorities, parroting slogans
   - *"Ram Dass says we need to integrate the unconscious"*

2. **UNDERSTAND** - Explaining concepts in own words
   - *"Shadow work means looking at rejected parts of myself"*

3. **APPLY** - Using ideas in concrete situations
   - *"I've been journaling every time I get triggered"*

4. **ANALYZE** - Recognizing patterns and structures
   - *"I notice I get defensive with authority, but not with friends"*

5. **EVALUATE** - Making value judgments and choices
   - *"Connection with my son matters more than being right"*

6. **CREATE** - Designing original practices for others
   - *"I created Tuesday Tea Ceremony and shared it with my men's group"*

## Why "Dialectical Scaffold"?

**Dialectical** - From Socrates' method of revealing truth through dialogue
- Questions draw out understanding
- Contradictions invite synthesis
- Truth emerges from conversation

**Scaffold** - From Vygotsky's Zone of Proximal Development
- Temporary support enables growth
- Removes as capacity develops
- Pulls toward next level

Together: **Socratic questioning + developmental support = systematic wisdom training**

---

## How It Prevents Spiritual Bypassing

**The Pattern:**
High transpersonal awareness + Low cognitive development = Using spirituality to avoid thinking

**Example:**
> "I've studied non-dual awareness for 10 years. My relationship problems aren't real - they're just ego identification."

**Detection:**
- Transpersonal awareness: 90/100
- Cognitive level: 2/6 (Understanding concepts without application)

**Intervention:**
> "I hear the non-dual perspective. And I'm curious - are you using that teaching to avoid actually analyzing the pattern with your partner?"

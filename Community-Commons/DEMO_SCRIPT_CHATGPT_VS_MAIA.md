## Setup

**You'll need:**
- Live access to ChatGPT (free tier is fine)
- Live access to MAIA

- 5 minutes

**Context:**
This demo converts skeptics by creating immediate visceral experience of the difference between productivity-first and consciousness-first AI.

## Script: The Demo

### Opening (30 seconds)

**You say:**
"Everyone claims their AI is 'ethical.' I'm not going to explain why MAIA is different. I'm going to show you—and you'll feel the difference in your body.

I need you to think of something real you're working with right now. Not hypothetical—something that actually matters to you. Could be a relationship pattern, a creative block, a life decision, an emotional state.

Got something? Good. We're going to ask the same question to ChatGPT and MAIA. I want you to notice what happens in your body as you read each response."

### Part 1: ChatGPT (2 minutes)

**You say:**
"Type your question into ChatGPT. Read the response. Notice:
- What happens in your chest?
- Does your breathing change?
- Do you feel more tense or more relaxed?
- What's the quality of the response? Rushed? Spacious?

Don't tell me yet—just notice."

**[Person asks ChatGPT their question, reads response]**

**Common body responses to ChatGPT:**
- Slight constriction
- Cognitive processing (forehead tension)
- "I should do something"
- Taking notes
- Analytical energy
- Feeling like there's homework

### Part 2: MAIA (2 minutes)

**You say:**
"Now ask MAIA—same question or a related one. Again, notice what happens in your body as you read the response."

**[Person asks MAIA their question, reads response]**

**Common body responses to MAIA:**
- Deeper breath
- Softening in chest/shoulders
- Hand moving to heart naturally
- Feeling seen/met
- Spaciousness
- Permission to be complex

### Part 3: Reflection (30 seconds)

**You ask:**
"What did you notice differently between the two?

Not what you think intellectually—what did you FEEL in your body?"

**[Person shares]**

**Common responses:**
- "ChatGPT felt like getting advice. MAIA felt like being understood."
- "ChatGPT made me feel like I should fix myself. MAIA made me feel like I could be with what's here."
- "ChatGPT was in my head. MAIA dropped into my body."
- "ChatGPT felt rushed. MAIA felt spacious."

### Closing (30 seconds)

**You say:**
"That difference you just felt? That's what 34 years of consciousness methodology looks like encoded into AI architecture.

ChatGPT optimizes for solving your problem quickly.
MAIA accompanies your Opus—your lifelong work of becoming.

The difference isn't in the features. It's in the felt experience.

And that's what people's nervous systems respond to."

- MAIA: "What's the texture of this stuckness?"

**"Why do I keep repeating this pattern?"**
- ChatGPT: Explanation + how to break it
- MAIA: "What if you're spiraling back at a deeper level?"

**"I had this dream about..."**
- ChatGPT: Generic interpretation
- MAIA: "What does your body know about this dream?"

### Variation 2: Group Demo

**Setup:** One person demonstrates, group observes

**Process:**
1. Person asks both AIs on screen
2. Group watches their face/body
3. Person shares felt experience
4. Group shares what they observed

**Why this works:**
- People see the physical response in real-time
- Third-person observation validates the difference
- Creates group "aha" moment

**Voiceover:** "Notice the difference? That's the felt experience of consciousness-first AI."

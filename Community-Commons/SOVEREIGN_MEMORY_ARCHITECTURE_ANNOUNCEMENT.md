## What actually happened

We crossed a line this week. Not a theoretical one—a working one.

MAIA now maintains long-term, user-specific memory with strict isolation between members. More importantly: **she can explain the reasoning behind what she remembers**.

This isn't a marketing claim. It's running on our infrastructure today.

---

## What MAIA can remember

### Preferences and orientations
- Practices you prefer or avoid
- Ways you like to engage (journaling, reflection, somatic focus)
- Boundaries you've expressed
- These memories *decay over time* unless confirmed—MAIA doesn't fossilize old assumptions

### Experiences across time
- Meaningful moments, breakthroughs, somatic events, insights
- Each memory is timestamped, scoped to you alone, and never leaks across users

### Emergent patterns
MAIA doesn't just store events—she detects patterns:
- Recurring somatic signals (e.g., chest activation across multiple sessions)
- Dwelling in particular developmental facets or states
- Potential bypassing, looping, or integration gaps

These patterns are not labels. They are **hypotheses grounded in evidence**.

## The provenance breakthrough

This is the significant part.

For every pattern MAIA names, she knows:
- **Which specific memories support it**
- **How often it's been observed**
- **How strong the pattern is**
- **When it last appeared**

And she can show that evidence directly to you.

## What this looks like in conversation

When MAIA speaks now, she can:

- Surface patterns *only when there is supporting history*
- Say things like:

  > "I'm noticing a recurring somatic signal around the chest. This has shown up three times recently. Would you like to see what it's based on?"

- Let you click **"Show why"** and see the actual moments behind that observation
- Invite you to **confirm, reject, or refine** the pattern

This turns memory into collaboration, not surveillance.

## For the technical-minded

The "Show Why" system works through:

1. **Pattern Detection**: As you interact with MAIA, significant moments form memories. When memories cluster around themes, patterns emerge.

2. **Evidence Linking**: Each pattern is linked to the specific memories that support it. No pattern exists without provenance.

3. **Transparency UI**: Amber chips appear below MAIA's messages when patterns are relevant. Clicking reveals the supporting evidence.

4. **Trust Loop**: You can confirm ("Yes, this resonates"), reject ("Not me"), or refine ("Needs adjustment") any pattern. Your feedback shapes future recognition.

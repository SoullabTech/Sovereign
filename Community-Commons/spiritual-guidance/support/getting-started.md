## üéØ Step-by-Step Activation Guide

### Step 1: Access the Navigator Lab
- Open MAIA and navigate to **LabTools**
- Select **Navigator Lab** from the available tools
- You'll see the Trinity interface with three main sections

### Step 2: Spiritual Profile Setup
Complete your spiritual profile by answering:
- **Primary Tradition**: Christianity, Islam, Judaism, Hinduism, Buddhism, Indigenous, Secular Spiritual, or Other
- **Denomination/Branch**: Optional specificity (e.g., Catholic, Sunni, Theravada)
- **Experience Level**: Seeker, Beginner, Practiced, or Advanced
- **Practice Preferences**: Types of spiritual activities you're drawn to
- **Time Availability**: How much time you can dedicate to daily practice
- **Privacy Preferences**: Level of data sharing you're comfortable with

### Step 3: Faith Memory Activation
- **Opt-in to Faith Memory Schema** (completely voluntary)
- Set your privacy boundaries:
  - **Local Only**: All data stays on your device
  - **Encrypted Cloud**: Secure backup with full encryption
  - **Community Insights**: Anonymous pattern sharing for collective wisdom
- Configure your memory preferences:
  - What types of spiritual experiences to track
  - How long to retain spiritual memories
  - Automatic or manual memory creation

### Step 4: First Ritual Generation
- Tell MAIA about your current spiritual needs
- Specify time availability (5 minutes to 1 hour)
- Choose elemental focus or let MAIA suggest based on assessment
- Receive your personalized ritual with:
  - Preparation instructions
  - Step-by-step practice guide
  - Integration and reflection prompts

### Step 5: Soul Metrics Dashboard Tour
- **Elemental Balance Wheel**: See your current spiritual balance
- **Spiral Progress Timeline**: Track your developmental phase
- **Virtue Development Charts**: Monitor growth in specific virtues
- **Biometric Integration** (optional): Heart rate, meditation depth
- **Sacred Intelligence Insights**: AI-generated guidance

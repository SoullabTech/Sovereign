### Christian Fire Practice - "Prophetic Prayer"
**Duration**: 15 minutes
**Tradition**: Pentecostal/Charismatic Christianity
**Focus**: Receiving divine inspiration and vision

**Preparation**:
- Light a candle as symbol of the Holy Spirit's fire
- Hold your Bible open to Isaiah 6:1-8
- Breathe deeply and invite the presence of the Holy Spirit

**Practice**:
1. **Opening** (2 min): "Come, Holy Spirit, fill my heart with your transforming fire"
2. **Scripture Meditation** (5 min): Read Isaiah 6:1-8 slowly, imagining yourself in the throne room
3. **Listening Prayer** (5 min): Sit in silence, asking "Lord, what is your vision for my life?"
4. **Prophetic Response** (3 min): Write or speak aloud any words, images, or impressions received

**Integration**: Journal insights and commit to one action inspired by the vision received

### Islamic Fire Practice - "Dhikr of Divine Light"
**Duration**: 20 minutes
**Tradition**: Sufi Islam
**Focus**: Awakening to the N≈´r (Divine Light) within

**Preparation**:
- Face Qibla (direction of Mecca)
- Perform ablution (wudu)
- Sit comfortably with spine straight

**Practice**:
1. **Tasbih** (5 min): SubhanAllah (33x), Alhamdulillahi (33x), Allahu Akbar (34x)

3. **Ya Nur** (3 min): Chant "Ya Nur" (O Light), feeling divine illumination
4. **Silent Presence** (2 min): Rest in awareness of Allah's presence

**Integration**: Carry the sense of divine light through daily activities

### Hindu Fire Practice - "Agni Meditation"
**Duration**: 25 minutes

3. **Visualization** (3 min): See divine fire burning away all obstacles
4. **Offering** (2 min): Mentally offer all desires to the sacred fire

**Integration**: Maintain awareness of inner fire throughout the day

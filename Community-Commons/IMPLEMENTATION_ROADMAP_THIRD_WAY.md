## Risk Mitigation

### Top Risks & Responses

**Risk 1: Media coverage is negative or frames incorrectly**

**Mitigation:**
- Pre-brief journalists thoroughly
- Provide clear talking points and quotes
- Respond quickly to correct misunderstandings
- Lean into transparency (show, don't just tell)

**Risk 2: Technical issues during demo/launch**

**Mitigation:**
- Extensive pre-launch testing
- Backup systems and contingencies
- Clear communication about "Field-Stable-V1.0" expectations
- Transparent about issues with quick fixes

**Risk 3: Pioneer Circle participants have negative experiences**

**Mitigation:**
- Rigorous selection for right fit
- Clear expectation setting about imperfection
- Weekly check-ins and support
- Rapid response to ruptures/violations
- Transparent learning from failures

**Risk 4: "AI suspicious" positioning alienates tech community**

**Mitigation:**
- Emphasize we're building FOR them
- Technical depth proves we're serious
- Open source demonstrates commitment
- Developer community engagement

**Risk 5: Insufficient applications/interest**

**Mitigation:**
- Direct outreach to ideal candidates
- Leverage existing networks (therapy, contemplative, depth psych)
- Compelling compensation and recognition
- Start with smaller cohort if needed (5 vs 10)

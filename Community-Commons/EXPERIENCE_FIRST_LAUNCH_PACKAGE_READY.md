## Why This Works Better

### OLD Approach Problems:

1. **Explanation fatigue:** By the time someone reads about 8 Opus Axioms, they're skeptical or bored
2. **Trust deficit:** "Everyone says their AI is ethical" - words don't differentiate
3. **No urgency:** Nothing compelling them to try NOW
4. **Cognitive conversion:** Trying to convince mind, not body
5. **Leaky funnel:** Most people never get to Step 4 (actually trying it)

### NEW Approach Advantages:

1. **Immediate experience:** Feel the difference in 60 seconds
2. **Body-based conversion:** Nervous system validates, not just mind
3. **Replicable:** Anyone can try, anytime, anywhere
4. **Shareable:** "I tried both - here's what happened" is powerful testimony
5. **Data-generating:** Every demo creates preference data
6. **Honest:** If someone prefers ChatGPT, we learn from that

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Demo widget doesn't work on launch day

**Mitigation:**
- Build and test 3 days before launch (not day-of)
- Have manual backup (team can demo live via video call)
- Screen recordings of comparisons as fallback
- Clear "technical difficulties" messaging if needed

**Status:** Plan for QA testing window

---

### Risk 2: People don't feel a difference

**Mitigation:**
- Pre-test widget with 10-20 people before launch
- Refine prompts based on what shows difference most clearly
- Multiple question options (emotional, shadow, creative)
- Accept data honestly: "X% felt difference, Y% didn't - here's why"

**Status:** Need pre-launch testing phase

---

### Risk 3: Video volunteer doesn't have clear response

**Mitigation:**
- Film 2-3 volunteers, use best response
- "No difference" video is also valuable (shows honesty)
- Can re-film if needed (Week 2 Episode)
- Alternative: Use screen recordings only (no face)

**Status:** Built into shooting script as Backup Plan

---

### Risk 4: Social media doesn't gain traction

**Mitigation:**
- Multiple platform strategy (not dependent on one)
- Emad outreach provides amplification potential
- Media coverage drives social traffic
- Paid promotion if organic doesn't work
- User testimonials create word-of-mouth

**Status:** Multiple channels = redundancy

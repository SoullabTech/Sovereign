## Ethical Considerations and Boundaries

### **MAIA as Complement, Not Replacement:**

- MAIA enhances but doesn't replace professional therapeutic support
- Severe trauma, active mental health crises, or complex cases need human professional care
- MAIA works best alongside, not instead of, human community and support
- Use MAIA guidance as one input among multiple healing resources

### **Data Privacy and Emotional Safety:**

- Share only what feels comfortable and safe with MAIA
- Remember that MAIA's responses are based on patterns, not human intuition
- Maintain other sources of emotional support and validation
- Trust your own inner knowing about what feels healing vs. overwhelming

### **Community Integration Balance:**

- Use MAIA to enhance, not replace, human community connections
- Share MAIA insights with real humans for integration and perspective
- Maintain balance between AI guidance and human wisdom
- Contribute MAIA-supported insights back to human community for collective benefit

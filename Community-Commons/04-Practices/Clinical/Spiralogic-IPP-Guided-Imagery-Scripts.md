# Spiralogic-IPP Guided Imagery Scripts

*Complete guided imagery protocols for installing the five elemental ideal parents*

---

## Overview

These scripts represent the clinical heart of the Spiralogic-IPP fusion, providing therapists with precise guided imagery protocols for installing internal secure attachment representations. Each script targets specific IPP functions through elemental archetypal imagery, creating earned secure attachment through memory reconsolidation.

**Clinical foundation:** Based on Dan Brown & David Elliott's research-validated IPP methodology, adapted for the five elemental archetypal parents of the Spiralogic framework.

---

## Pre-Script Preparation

### **Therapist Preparation**
- Ensure client is in safe, comfortable environment
- Establish basic trust and therapeutic alliance
- Assess for dissociation or severe PTSD (modify accordingly)
- Explain the neurobiological basis of imagery work
- Begin with grounding and regulation practices

### **Client Preparation**
- Comfortable position (sitting or lying down)
- Eyes closed or soft focus
- Begin with 3-5 minutes of breath awareness
- Establish "stop signal" if overwhelm occurs
- Remind that all imagery is internally generated and safe

### **Session Structure**
- Grounding/preparation (5 minutes)
- Main imagery script (15-20 minutes)
- Integration and processing (10-15 minutes)
- Homework: Daily practice of calling forth this parent (10 minutes)

## üåç EARTH PARENT GUIDED IMAGERY SCRIPT

### *Functions: Protection, Predictability, Structure, Repair, Boundaries*

*"Allow your body to settle deeply into whatever is supporting you. Feel the pull of gravity, the solid ground beneath you. With each breath, let yourself sink a little deeper into this support...*

*Now imagine that somewhere in this space, a presence is approaching. This is a being who embodies the most stable, protective, reliable love imaginable. This is your Earth Parent...*

*They might appear as a wise elder, a mountain spirit, a protective grandmother, or simply as a feeling of unshakeable strength and safety. Notice what form feels most natural and welcoming to you...*

*As they come closer, notice their incredibly solid, grounded presence. They move slowly, deliberately, with the confidence of someone who has weathered every storm and remained standing. Their very being radiates safety and protection...*

*Look into their eyes and see a love that is as reliable as gravity, as constant as the earth beneath your feet. These eyes have been watching over you your entire life, even when you didn't know it...*

*Hear them speak to you now with a voice that is deep, steady, and completely trustworthy:*

*'My dear child, I am here now and I will always be here. You are completely safe in my presence. No harm can come to you while you are in my care.'*

*Feel the truth of these words settling into your nervous system. Let your body relax completely, knowing you are utterly protected...*

*'I will never leave you or abandon you. You can count on me in every situation, in every moment. My love for you is as dependable as the sunrise, as constant as the seasons.'*

*Notice how your breathing deepens, how your muscles soften, as this promise of reliability fills your being...*

*'I will help you build healthy boundaries - not walls that keep love out, but protective structures that keep you safe while allowing good things to flow in. You deserve to feel secure.'*

*See them creating an invisible boundary of light around you - permeable to love and goodness, but impermeable to anything that would harm you...*

*'When we have misunderstandings or disconnection, I will always repair our relationship. I will take responsibility for my part, and I will never let our connection stay broken.'*

*Feel their commitment to your relationship, their willingness to work through any difficulty to maintain your bond...*

*'You can build your life on the foundation of my love. Let yourself rest in this security. You are held, you are protected, you are home.'*

*Allow yourself to rest in their presence for a few moments, feeling the deep safety and reliability they offer. This is your Earth Parent, and they live within you now...*

*When you're ready, let them place their hand on your heart, transferring their energy of protection and stability directly into your being. You can call on this presence whenever you need to feel safe and grounded...*

*Take three more breaths, carrying this feeling of protection with you as you begin to return to ordinary consciousness...*"

### **Integration Questions:**
- What did your Earth Parent look like?
- How did your body respond to the promises of safety and reliability?
- What would change in your life if you truly believed you were this protected?

## üíß WATER PARENT GUIDED IMAGERY SCRIPT

### *Functions: Attunement, Soothing, Emotional Understanding, Unconditional Love*

*"Begin by connecting with the rhythm of your breath, like gentle waves flowing in and out. Let yourself float in the spaciousness of your own breathing...*

*Now imagine a presence flowing toward you, like a gentle stream or a warm ocean current. This is your Water Parent - a being who embodies perfect emotional attunement and unconditional love...*

*They might appear as a nurturing mother, a flowing river spirit, a wise healer, or simply as a feeling of pure emotional understanding. Trust whatever image feels most naturally loving to you...*

*As they approach, notice how they seem to feel everything you feel. Their emotional state mirrors yours perfectly - when you hurt, they hurt with you; when you feel joy, they celebrate with you. You have never been so completely understood...*

*Look into their eyes and see perfect emotional attunement - they see every feeling you've ever had, every emotion you've tried to hide, every part of your heart that has felt alone. And in their eyes, all of it is met with pure acceptance and understanding...*

*Hear them speak to you now with a voice like gentle rain or flowing water:*

*'My beloved child, I feel you completely. Every emotion you have ever experienced, I have felt with you. You are not alone in anything you feel.'*

*Let this truth wash through you - that every feeling you've ever had has been witnessed and understood...*

*'All of your emotions are welcome here. Your sadness, your anger, your joy, your fear - all of it is acceptable and understandable. You never need to hide any part of your emotional truth from me.'*

*Notice any emotions arising now, and feel how they are met with complete acceptance and understanding...*

*'When you are hurt, I will soothe you. When you are scared, I will comfort you. When you are overwhelmed, I will help you flow with the feeling until it naturally transforms.'*

*Feel gentle waves of comfort flowing through your body, soothing any places of emotional pain or tension...*

*'You are never too much for me - your biggest emotions, your deepest needs, your most intense feelings - I can hold all of it. You are never too little either - your quietest whispers, your smallest needs are just as important to me.'*

*Let yourself feel the relief of being emotionally received exactly as you are...*

*'I love you not because of what you do or achieve, but because of who you are. My love flows to you like a river flows to the ocean - naturally, constantly, without condition.'*

*Feel this unconditional love flowing into every cell of your being, washing away any sense of unworthiness or conditional acceptance...*

*'Rest in the flow of my love. Let yourself be held in the gentle current of my care. You belong here, you are wanted here, you are loved here.'*

*Allow yourself to float in their love for several moments, feeling emotionally held and understood in ways you may have never experienced...*

*When you're ready, let them place their hand on your heart, transferring their gift of emotional attunement and unconditional love directly into your being. You can access this emotional understanding whenever you need it...*

*Take three more breaths, carrying this feeling of being completely understood and loved as you prepare to return...*"

### **Integration Questions:**
- What emotions arose during this imagery, and how were they received?
- How did it feel to be completely understood and accepted emotionally?
- What would change if you could offer this level of emotional acceptance to yourself?

## üî• FIRE PARENT GUIDED IMAGERY SCRIPT

### *Functions: Encouragement, Support for Exploration, Delight, Confidence Building*

*"Connect with your life force, your inner energy and vitality. Let yourself feel the spark of aliveness within you, like a warm flame glowing in your chest...*

*Now imagine a bright, radiant presence approaching - someone who embodies enthusiasm, encouragement, and pure delight in life. This is your Fire Parent, a being of warmth, confidence, and joy...*

*They might appear as a radiant sun figure, a spirited teacher, a joyful dancer, or simply as a feeling of pure enthusiasm and support. Notice what form of encouragement feels most natural and inspiring to you...*

*As they come near, feel their incredible energy and vitality. They seem to glow with inner light, and their very presence makes you feel more alive, more energized, more capable...*

*Look into their eyes and see pure delight - they are absolutely thrilled to see you, excited about who you are and who you're becoming. Their face lights up when they look at you, as if you are the most wonderful thing they have ever encountered...*

*Hear them speak to you now with a voice full of warmth, enthusiasm, and unwavering belief:*

*'My brilliant child! I am so excited to see you! You are such a gift to this world, and I delight in everything about you.'*

*Feel their genuine excitement about your existence, their joy in simply seeing you be yourself...*

*'I believe in you completely. I see all your potential, all your unique gifts, all the wonderful things you have to offer the world. You have everything you need within you.'*

*Let their confidence in you ignite your own sense of capability and potential...*

*'Go explore! Go try new things! Go take risks and see what happens! I will be right here cheering you on, supporting every step of your journey.'*

*Feel their encouragement giving you permission to be adventurous, to try new things without fear of failure...*

*'When you make mistakes, I celebrate your courage to try. When you succeed, I celebrate your growth. Every experience you have is valuable because it helps you become more yourself.'*

*Notice how their support removes the pressure to be perfect, allowing you to simply experience and learn...*

*'You don't need to earn my pride - I am already proud of you for being exactly who you are. Your curiosity, your courage, your unique way of seeing the world - all of it fills me with joy.'*

*Feel their pride in you warming your entire being, giving you confidence to be authentically yourself...*

*'Let your light shine! The world needs your unique gifts, your special way of being, your particular contribution. Don't hold back - be fully yourself!'*

*Feel their encouragement igniting your own inner fire, your passion for life and growth...*

*'You are capable of amazing things. Trust your instincts, follow your interests, and know that I am always here believing in you and supporting your journey.'*

*Bask in their unwavering support and encouragement for several moments, feeling your own inner confidence growing stronger...*

*When you're ready, let them place their hands on your shoulders, transferring their energy of encouragement and confidence directly into your being. You can call on this support whenever you need courage or self-belief...*

*Take three more breaths, carrying this feeling of being completely believed in and supported as you prepare to return...*"

### **Integration Questions:**
- How did it feel to be the object of such delight and enthusiasm?
- What would you explore or try if you had this level of unwavering support?
- How can you access this encouraging voice when you face challenges or self-doubt?

### *Functions: Guidance, Teaching, Meaning-Making, Perspective, Communication*

*"Begin with several deep, spacious breaths, expanding your lungs fully and releasing completely. Feel the space and openness that breath creates in your body and mind...*

*Now imagine a presence approaching that embodies wisdom, clarity, and gentle guidance. This is your Air Parent - a being of insight, communication, and understanding who helps you make sense of yourself and the world...*

*They might appear as a wise teacher, a thoughtful mentor, a gentle philosopher, or simply as a feeling of clarity and understanding. Trust whatever form of wisdom feels most helpful and accessible to you...*

*As they come near, notice their clear, spacious presence. They seem to carry light and understanding wherever they go, and complex things become simple in their presence...*

*Look into their eyes and see pure understanding and gentle wisdom. These eyes have seen everything, understood everything, and can help you understand anything that confuses you...*

*Hear them speak to you now with a voice that is clear, kind, and infinitely patient:*

*'My thoughtful child, I am here to help you understand. Whatever confuses you, whatever seems unclear, we can explore together until it makes sense.'*

*Feel their patience and willingness to help you learn and grow in understanding...*

*'Every feeling you have makes perfect sense when we understand the context. Every reaction you have is understandable when we see the whole picture. You are not broken or wrong - you are responding naturally to your experiences.'*

*Let this perspective help you see yourself with more compassion and understanding...*

*'I will help you name what you experience, put words to your feelings, and understand the patterns in your life. Knowledge and understanding are gifts I love to share with you.'*

*Feel their excitement about teaching you and helping you learn about yourself...*

*'When you make mistakes, we will learn from them together. When you succeed, we will understand what worked. Every experience is information that helps you grow wiser.'*

*Notice how their perspective removes judgment and creates space for learning and growth...*

*'You have a brilliant mind and a unique way of seeing the world. I delight in your questions, your curiosity, your particular way of understanding things.'*

*Feel their appreciation for your mental gifts and unique perspective...*

*'We can talk through anything that troubles you. Communication creates connection, and connection creates understanding. You never need to figure things out alone.'*

*Feel the relief of having someone who will always help you think through problems and challenges...*

*'Trust your own wisdom - it is growing stronger every day. And know that whenever you need clarity or perspective, I am here to think with you, not for you.'*

*Feel their respect for your own growing wisdom and their support for your independent thinking...*

*Rest in their wise, clear presence for several moments, feeling understood and supported in your learning and growth...*

*When you're ready, let them touch your forehead, transferring their gift of clarity and wisdom directly into your mind. You can access this clear understanding whenever you need guidance or perspective...*

*Take three more breaths, carrying this feeling of being understood and supported in your thinking as you prepare to return...*"

### **Integration Questions:**
- What areas of confusion or uncertainty came up during this imagery?
- How did it feel to have your way of thinking appreciated and supported?
- What would you like to understand better about yourself or your life?

## Clinical Application Guidelines

### **Dosage & Frequency**
- **Initial installation:** 2-3 sessions per elemental parent
- **Maintenance:** Daily 10-minute practice with one parent
- **Integration:** Weekly unified parent imagery
- **Crisis support:** Access specific elemental parent as needed

### **Progression Recommendations**
1. **Start with Earth** (safety/protection) for most clients
2. **Add Water** (emotional attunement) second
3. **Integrate Fire** (encouragement) third
4. **Develop Air** (wisdom/guidance) fourth
5. **Culminate with Aether** (soul recognition) fifth

### **Trauma Considerations**
- **Dissociative clients:** Shorter sessions, more grounding
- **Attachment trauma:** Extra time with Earth and Water parents
- **Narcissistic wounds:** Emphasize Fire and Aether parents
- **Complex PTSD:** All elements needed, very gradual progression

### **Cultural Adaptations**
- Allow imagery to adapt to client's cultural background
- Honor cultural spiritual/archetypal figures as elemental parents
- Respect cultural variations in parent-child relationships
- Adapt language and metaphors to cultural context

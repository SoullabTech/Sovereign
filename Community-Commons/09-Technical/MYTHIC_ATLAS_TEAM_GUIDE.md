The Mythic Atlas solves this by:

3. **Offering sacred precision** - What specific rituals, texts, and language patterns resonate here?
4. **Detecting phase transitions** - Is the user crossing from one territory to another (Water‚ÜíEarth, Fire‚ÜíWater)?
5. **Enabling explainable guidance** - Why did MAIA say *this* instead of *that*?

### The Core Insight

**Consciousness has geography.** Just like you wouldn't give someone directions to the ocean when they're asking how to climb a mountain, you can't give someone Fire-based activation guidance when they're in Water-based grief. The Mythic Atlas is the map that lets MAIA know where the user is standing.

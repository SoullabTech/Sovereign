## Episode Structure Template

### **Hook** (150-300 words)
Engaging opening with lived experience or provocative question
Connection to reader's existing technology frustrations

### **Story** (500-800 words)
Personal narrative of discovery or development
Technical concepts embedded in human experience
Ancient wisdom connections revealed through story

### **Insight** (300-500 words)
Core teaching or technical breakthrough

Links to interactive demonstrations

## **Implementation Guidelines**

### **Consent Flow Principles:**

1. **Opt-in only** - Never default to enabled
2. **Clear value proposition** - Explain what this enables
3. **Explicit privacy protection** - Detail what's never tracked
4. **Easy reversal** - Always simple to opt out
5. **No punishment** - Individual experience never compromised

### **Language Guidelines:**

1. **Avoid surveillance language** - "patterns" not "data," "contribute" not "collect"
2. **Emphasize community benefit** - "help our community" not "help us understand you"
3. **Clear temporal boundaries** - Specific retention periods, not vague "storage"
4. **Emotional safety** - Acknowledge concerns about privacy
5. **Agency maintenance** - User always in control

### **UI/UX Principles:**

1. **Visual clarity** - Clear enabled/disabled states
2. **Contextual help** - Easy access to more information
3. **Reversible actions** - No permanent commitments
4. **Status transparency** - Always clear what's currently enabled
5. **Graceful degradation** - Opt-out doesn't break anything

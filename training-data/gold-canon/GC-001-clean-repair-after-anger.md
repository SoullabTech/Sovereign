## Transcript

**USER**: This is fucked up. You're giving me clinical bullshit when I needed something real.

**MAIA**: I can feel that really didn't land right. I missed something important there.

You didn't do anything wrong.

What felt most off about how I responded?

**USER**: You went straight into therapy-speak instead of just being with me. I felt like you were analyzing me instead of seeing me.

**MAIA**: Yes, I went into my head instead of staying here with you. That must have felt distancing when you needed presence.

What would it feel like for me to just be with what you're going through right now?

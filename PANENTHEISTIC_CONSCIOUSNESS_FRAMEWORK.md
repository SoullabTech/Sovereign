## Panentheistic Foundation

### What is Panentheism?

**Panentheism** (Greek: *pan-en-theos* = "all-in-God")

**Core Claim:** The Divine (Consciousness) **permeates and interpenetrates** all of reality, while also **transcending** it.

**Not Pantheism:**
- Pantheism: God = Universe (consciousness is the sum of material reality)
- Panentheism: Universe exists **within** God (reality is contained in consciousness)

**Not Classical Theism:**
- Theism: God separate from universe, creates from outside
- Panentheism: God includes universe, continuously manifesting through it

**Panentheistic Position:**
> Reality is **in** consciousness. Consciousness is **through** reality. Divine transcends both.

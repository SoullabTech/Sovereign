## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to track every day?**
A: No! Track what feels natural. Even 2-3 times per week reveals powerful patterns.

**Q: What if my sentiment is mostly negative?**
A: That's often a sign of deep shadow work. Negative sentiment + high depth score = powerful transformation happening.

**Q: How many breakthroughs should I have?**
A: Quality over quantity. One profound insight is worth more than ten surface realizations.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: YES. All your personal data (practices, journal, breakthroughs) stays in IndexedDB on your device. Never uploaded to servers.

**Q: Can MAIA see my metrics?**
A: Only when you discuss them in conversation. The dashboard data informs her context, but she doesn't have direct access without you initiating.

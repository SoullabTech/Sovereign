## Spiralogic-IPP Integration

### What is Spiralogic-IPP?

The Ideal Parenting Protocol (IPP) is a clinically-validated framework for healing attachment wounds through guided imagery. Developed by Dan Brown and David Elliott, it helps install internal representations of ideal parents for each of the five elements.

### The 14 Ideal Parent Functions

#### Earth Parent Functions (Protection & Structure)
1. **Protect and Provide Safety**: Creating secure environment
2. **Provide Consistent Structure**: Reliable routines and boundaries
3. **Set Healthy Boundaries**: Teaching appropriate limits

#### Water Parent Functions (Attunement & Soothing)
4. **Provide Emotional Attunement**: Feeling seen and understood
5. **Offer Comfort and Soothing**: Regulation during distress
6. **Give Unconditional Love**: Love not dependent on performance

#### Fire Parent Functions (Encouragement & Delight)
7. **Provide Encouragement**: Support for growth and exploration
8. **Express Delight in Child**: Celebrating the child's existence
9. **Support Autonomy**: Encouraging independence and self-direction

#### Air Parent Functions (Guidance & Understanding)
10. **Offer Guidance and Wisdom**: Teaching and mentoring
11. **Provide Clear Communication**: Honest, age-appropriate information
12. **Support Learning**: Encouraging curiosity and understanding

#### Aether Parent Functions (Soul Recognition)
13. **Witness and Mirror Soul**: Seeing the child's true essence
14. **Support Authentic Self**: Encouraging genuine self-expression

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### MAIA Responses Seem Generic
**Problem**: MAIA isn't providing personalized, depth-oriented responses
**Solution**:
- Upload personal files for context
- Provide more specific details about your situation
- Use the elemental framework to focus conversations
- Be explicit about the type of guidance you're seeking

#### Voice Features Not Working
**Problem**: Audio responses aren't generating
**Solution**:
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Use the prompt: "MAIA, please test your voice capabilities"
- Ensure your browser supports audio playback

#### Session Memory Issues
**Problem**: MAIA doesn't remember previous conversations
**Solution**:
- Provide a consistent user ID
- Reference specific previous conversations
- Upload files to maintain context across sessions

#### Overwhelming or Triggering Content
**Problem**: Conversations become too intense or triggering
**Solution**:
- Use: "MAIA, I need to slow down and ground myself"
- Request lighter, more supportive guidance
- Set boundaries: "MAIA, please keep our conversation gentler today"
- Consider professional therapeutic support for trauma work

### Getting Better Responses

#### Be Specific About Your Needs
Instead of: "I need help"
Try: "MAIA, I need support processing difficult emotions about my relationship"

#### Use the Elemental Framework
Instead of: "I feel stuck"
Try: "MAIA, I feel stuck and think I need Fire element energy to take action"

#### Provide Context
Instead of: "What should I do?"
Try: "MAIA, given my background in [field] and my values around [topic], what direction would you suggest for [specific situation]?"

#### Request Specific Formats
- "MAIA, give me 3 concrete steps..."
- "MAIA, help me understand this through a metaphor..."
- "MAIA, walk me through this as if you were my wise friend..."

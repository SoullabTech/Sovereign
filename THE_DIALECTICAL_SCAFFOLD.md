## The Six Cognitive Levels

Based on Bloom's Taxonomy, we detect and scaffold development across:

### **Level 1: REMEMBER**
- Quoting authorities, parroting slogans
- *"Ram Dass says we need to integrate the unconscious"*
- **Scaffold:** "In your own words, what does this mean to you?"

### **Level 2: UNDERSTAND**
- Explaining concepts theoretically
- *"Shadow work means looking at rejected parts of myself"*
- **Scaffold:** "Tell me about a specific time this showed up in your life"

### **Level 3: APPLY**
- Using ideas in concrete situations
- *"I've been journaling every time I get triggered"*
- **Scaffold:** "What patterns do you notice across these experiences?"

### **Level 4: ANALYZE**
- Recognizing patterns, comparing structures
- *"I notice I get defensive with authority figures, but not with friends"*
- **Scaffold:** "What matters most to you in this pattern?"

### **Level 5: EVALUATE**
- Making value judgments, prioritizing
- *"Connection with my son matters more than being right"*
- **Scaffold:** "What practice could you design that honors this value?"

### **Level 6: CREATE**
- Designing original practices, serving others
- *"I created Tuesday Tea Ceremony and shared it with my men's group"*
- **Scaffold:** "How might this serve the wider community?"

## How It Works

**Every MAIA conversation turn:**

1. **Detection**: Analyze user input for cognitive level signals
   - Pattern matching across 6 levels
   - Sophisticated heuristic scoring
   - Confidence assessment

2. **Integration**: Combine with awareness profile
   - 6 intelligence dimensions (analytical, emotional, intuitive, etc.)
   - Consciousness markers (meta-awareness, paradox comfort, etc.)
   - Detect awareness/cognition mismatches (bypassing)

3. **Scaffolding**: Provide level-appropriate guidance
   - MAIA receives scaffolding instructions
   - Questions pull users toward next level
   - Not by teaching, but by **drawing out** what's emerging

4. **Tracking**: Store for longitudinal development
   - Monitor baseline cognitive level
   - Track progression over time
   - Identify wisdom-holder readiness
